Frequently Asked Questions

How do I record a voice entry?

Tap the record button (the large circular button at the bottom of the screen). Speak naturally about what you accomplished. When finished, tap stop. Speakfolio's AI will automatically structure your recording into organized fields: Title, Summary, Context, Action, Result, and Impact.

Can I edit entries after recording?

Yes. After the AI structures your entry, you can edit any field before saving. You can also edit saved entries later from the History or entry detail screens. Each edit creates a new version, so you can always compare changes or revert to previous versions.

How does the streak system work?

Record at least one entry per day to maintain your streak. The streak counter shows on your Home dashboard. You'll receive optional streak warning notifications if you haven't recorded by evening. Streaks reset to zero if you miss a day.

What output formats can I generate?

Speakfolio can generate performance reviews, handover documents, and resume bullets from your entries. Export as PDF, Word document, or plain text. Free accounts get 5 AI-generated outputs per month. Premium accounts have unlimited generation.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS encryption. Voice recordings are stored in encrypted cloud storage. Our authentication is managed by SOC 2 Type II certified providers. We never sell your personal data. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings > scroll to the bottom > Delete Account. This permanently removes your account, all entries, voice recordings, and generated outputs from our servers. This action cannot be undone.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Open Settings on your iPhone > tap your name > Subscriptions > Speakfolio > Cancel Subscription. You'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period.

The app isn't recording audio. What should I do?

Make sure Speakfolio has microphone permission. Go to iPhone Settings > Speakfolio > toggle Microphone on. If the issue persists, force-close the app and reopen it. Still having trouble? Email us at support@speakfolio.app with your device model and iOS version.

System Requirements

  • iOS 16.0 or later
  • iPhone or iPad with microphone
  • Internet connection required for AI processing and sync
  • Approximately 50 MB of storage
