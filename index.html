Questions You're Probably Asking

I've tried brag docs before. They never stick. Why would this be different?
Brag docs fail because writing about yourself mid-flow feels awkward and performative. You're not going to stop what you're doing to type out accomplishments. SpeakFolio uses voice. 30 seconds of talking while the win is fresh, like you're telling a friend. No typing, no friction, no discipline required. That's why it actually sticks.
I genuinely don't have time to document my work.
30 seconds per win. That's it. Most users capture 2-3 wins per week in under 5 minutes total. The ROI is hours saved during review season, and the raises you'll actually negotiate for because you can prove what you did.
I don't want to sound like a bragger.
Neither do we. SpeakFolio structures your wins as professional documentation, not self-promotion. Factual: here's what happened, here's the outcome, here's who benefited. You're not bragging; you're keeping receipts. There's a difference.
