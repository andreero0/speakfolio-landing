Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 18, 2026

Introduction

Speakfolio ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect information you provide when creating an account:

  • Name and email address (via third-party authentication services)
  • Profile information (optional)

Voice Recordings

When you record voice notes:

  • Audio files are encrypted and stored securely on cloud storage services
  • Transcriptions are processed using AI transcription services
  • You can delete recordings at any time from within the app
  • Deleted recordings are permanently removed from our servers

Usage Data

We automatically collect:

  • Device information (type, operating system version)
  • App usage analytics (optional and anonymized)
  • Error logs (for debugging purposes only)
  • Feature usage patterns (to improve the app experience)

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Speakfolio service
  • Process your voice recordings and generate AI-powered outputs
  • Send you notifications (with your explicit permission)
  • Improve app performance and fix technical issues
  • Process subscription payments (via Apple App Store or Google Play Store)
  • Analyze app usage to enhance features and user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

Data Storage and Security

We take data security seriously and use industry-standard measures:

  • Authentication: Managed by SOC 2 Type II certified authentication providers
  • Database: Hosted on secure cloud database services with row-level security policies
  • Storage: Encrypted cloud storage for audio files
  • AI Processing: Third-party AI services for transcription and content generation
  • Transport: All data transmitted over HTTPS/TLS encryption

Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

We do NOT sell your personal information. We only share data with trusted service providers:

  • Authentication services: User account management and security
  • Cloud database providers: Data storage and hosting
  • AI transcription services: Voice-to-text conversion
  • AI generation services: Content creation and processing
  • Payment processors: Subscription and payment management
  • Analytics providers (optional): Anonymous usage analytics
  • Error tracking services (optional): Performance monitoring and debugging

These services are bound by their own privacy policies and data processing agreements.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Delete: Delete your account and all associated data at any time
  • Export: Download your data in machine-readable format (JSON)
  • Opt Out: Disable analytics tracking in app settings
  • Revoke: Withdraw microphone permissions via device settings
  • Rectify: Correct inaccurate personal information
  • Object: Object to certain data processing activities

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@speakfolio.app

Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Voice recordings: Deleted immediately when you delete entries
  • Transcriptions: Deleted with their associated entries
  • AI outputs: Retained until you manually delete them
  • Analytics: Aggregated and anonymized after 90 days
  • Error logs: Retained for 30 days for debugging purposes

Children's Privacy

Speakfolio is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate. By using Speakfolio, you consent to this transfer. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information we collect and how it's used
  • Right to request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Legal basis for processing: Consent and contract performance
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Speakfolio is a native mobile application and does not use traditional web cookies. However, we may use:

  • Local storage for app settings and cached data
  • Analytics SDKs that may use device identifiers
  • Session tokens for authentication

You can control tracking via your device settings and in-app analytics preferences.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy with a new "Last Updated" date
  • Sending an in-app notification
  • Requiring acceptance of the updated policy before continued use

Your continued use of Speakfolio after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Email: privacy@speakfolio.app

Support: support@speakfolio.app
